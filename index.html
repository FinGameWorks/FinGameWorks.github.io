EPOCH

a mobile procedrual generated space game

Procedrual Universe Experience

on Mobile Platform

EPOCH will take procedrual generation into mobile platforms, making a bigger world than every mobile space game, including planetary landing, many star systems to explore, fully procedrual generated.
EPOCH 将通过在移动平台上使用过程生成技术制作一个比以往所有手机游戏都广大的宇宙，包括可登陆行星和许多个太阳系大小的星系，所有这一切都是过程生成出来的。
